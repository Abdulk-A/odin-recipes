Fried Chicken

Bucket of Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season flour
  2. Dip chicken in buttermilk
  3. Dip Chicken in the seasoned flour
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 once more
  5. Have your oil preheated
  6. Fry chicken for 30 minutes
